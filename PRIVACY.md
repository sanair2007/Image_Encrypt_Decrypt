# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 12/04/2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this application (“the App”) handles user data. The App is designed to function without any external communication, data collection, or storage beyond the device itself.

## Data Collection

The App does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal or non-personal data**. Specifically:

* No personal information (such as name, email, or identifiers) is collected.
* No usage analytics or tracking is performed.
* No cookies, trackers, or similar technologies are used.

## Data Storage

* The App does **not store any data externally**.
* Any data generated during use remains **temporarily in memory** and is not persisted unless explicitly handled by the user within the device environment.
* The App does not create or manage files outside its local execution context.

## Network Communication

* The App does **not connect to the internet**.
* No data is sent to or received from external servers.
* All functionality is fully contained within the device.

## Third-Party Services

* The App does **not use any third-party services, APIs, or external libraries that communicate over a network**.

## Permissions

* The App does not request or require access to device features such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage beyond what is necessary to run the local webview.

## Security

* Since no data is collected or transmitted, risks related to data breaches or misuse are inherently minimized.
* Users retain full control over any data they choose to input or interact with locally.

## Children’s Privacy

* The App does not knowingly collect any information from users, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in the revised policy with an updated effective date.
